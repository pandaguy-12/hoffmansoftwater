The Problem

Untreated Water Destroys Industrial Equipment

Cincinnati's water hardness (115–135 mg/L) is in the second-highest national category. Without proper pretreatment, every system in your facility is compromised.

  • Boiler & Cooling Tower Scale

    Mineral buildup reduces heat transfer efficiency, increases fuel consumption, and accelerates tube failure in critical systems.

  • Unplanned Downtime & Repair Costs

    Scaled heating elements, fouled membranes, and corroded piping lead to emergency shutdowns and six-figure repair bills.

  • Process & Product Quality Failures

    Hard water compromises manufacturing consistency, pharmaceutical purity, and food processing compliance.

  • Energy Waste & Regulatory Risk

    Scaled systems consume up to 29% more energy. Wastewater discharge non-compliance carries fines and operational risk.
